Create the FinanceOS Analytics module as a complete Management Accounting and Cost Allocation workspace.

IMPORTANT:

This is NOT a marketing analytics dashboard.

This module is dedicated to:

- Cost accounting
- Cost centers
- Shared expense allocation
- Department profitability
- Client and project profitability
- Multi-entity cost allocation
- Management P&L reporting

The purpose of this module is to show where costs are truly consumed, regardless of which legal entity originally paid the expense.

For example:

An employee may be paid by CarDealer.ai but work for CarDealer.ai, T3 Marketing, TopMrktr and Smile More.

The accounting books show that CarDealer.ai paid the full cost.

The Analytics module must show the economic reality by reallocating that cost across the four companies based on a defined allocation rule.

--------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS RULES
--------------------------------------------------

1. Every allocation must total 100%.

2. Allocated amount plus remaining unallocated amount must equal the original expense.

3. An expense cannot be allocated twice within the same scenario.

4. Allocation rules must have effective dates.

5. Approved periods should be locked.

6. Allocation changes must be auditable.

7. Direct costs should not be allocated unless explicitly overridden.

8. Intercompany allocations should remain separate from legal accounting entries until formally posted.

9. Book results must always remain visible alongside adjusted analytical results.

10. Never overwrite original accounting data.

--------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT TERMINOLOGY
--------------------------------------------------

Use these terms consistently:

Book Amount:
The amount recorded in the accounting system.

Allocation Adjustment:
The analytical redistribution of a cost.

Adjusted Amount:
Book Amount plus or minus Allocation Adjustments.

Paying Entity:
The legal entity that paid the expense.

Consuming Entity:
The entity that economically used the resource.

Direct Cost:
A cost directly attributable to one entity, department, client or project.

Shared Cost:
A cost consumed by multiple entities, departments, clients or projects.

Allocation Driver:
The basis used to distribute a shared cost.

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL PRODUCT VISION
--------------------------------------------------

FinanceOS Analytics should answer:

- Which company actually consumed this expense?
- Which department truly costs the most?
- What is the real cost of the finance function?
- What is the true profitability of each entity?
- Which clients are profitable after overhead allocation?
- Which projects consume the most resources?
- How much cost is still unallocated?
- How would profitability change under another allocation method?

QuickBooks shows who paid the expense.

FinanceOS Analytics must show who actually consumed the expense.

Build the complete interface and routing structure, but do not connect to live accounting data yet unless an existing FinanceOS data source is already available.

Do not break or modify the existing Reporting, Budget, Forecast, Commission or Accounting modules.
