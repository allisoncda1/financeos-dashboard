====================================================
OBJECTIVE
====================================================

Migrate all financial history endpoints from Google Drive to FinanceOS Core.

The Dashboard must become a pure presentation layer.

FinanceOS Core is now the ONLY source of truth.

====================================================
SCOPE
====================================================

Migrate ONLY:

- getEntityFinancials()
- getEntityHistory()

and every endpoint depending on them.

Use ONLY:

financial_periods

through CORE_DATABASE_URL.

====================================================
DO NOT TOUCH
====================================================

- Customers
- Vendors
- Banking
- Anomalies
- AI
- Rules
- Reports generation
- React
- Frontend
- Google Drive fallback

====================================================
REQUIREMENTS
====================================================

Read exclusively from financial_periods.

Never rebuild historical calculations inside the Dashboard.

Never recompute:

- Revenue
- Expenses
- Net Income
- Gross Margin
- Cash
- Assets
- Liabilities
- Equity

Everything must come directly from FinanceOS Core.
