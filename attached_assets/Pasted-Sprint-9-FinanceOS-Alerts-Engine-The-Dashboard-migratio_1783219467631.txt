====================================================
OBJECTIVE
====================================================

Build the FinanceOS Alerts Engine inside FinanceOS Core.

Alerts are generated during every synchronization and stored in PostgreSQL.

The Dashboard becomes presentation only.

entity_id

scope
(entity / portfolio)

severity
(info | warning | critical)

category
(cash | revenue | ar | ap | margin | validation | sync | banking | accounting)

title

description

recommendation

status
(open | acknowledged | resolved)

created_at

resolved_at

rule_id

snapshot_id (optional)

metadata (jsonb)

====================================================
ALERT RULES
====================================================

Implement rule-based alert generation.

Initial rule set:

Cash

- Cash below configurable threshold

- Cash runway below X months

Revenue

- Revenue down more than configurable %

Margin

- Gross margin below threshold

- Net margin below threshold

Accounts Receivable

- AR overdue %

- DSO above threshold

- Large unpaid customer balance

Accounts Payable

- AP overdue %

- DPO above threshold

Validation

- Validation failures

- Missing required reports

Sync

- Sync failed

- Sync stale

Accounting

- Balance sheet does not balance

- Negative equity

Business

- Negative net income

- Large month-over-month variance
